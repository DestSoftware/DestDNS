Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how DEST DNS ("we", "our", or "the app") handles user information, the purpose of required permissions, and our commitment to protecting your privacy.

1. Data Collection and Usage

DEST DNS is built with a "Privacy First" approach. Our data handling practices are as follows:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or device contacts.
  • No Registration: Users are not required to create an account or provide any login credentials to use the application.

2. Android VpnService Usage

To provide its core functionality, DEST DNS utilizes the Android VpnService API. It is important to understand how this is used:

  • Purpose: The VPN service is used exclusively to intercept and redirect your DNS queries (the requests made to resolve website names into IP addresses) to the secure DNS providers you select within the app.
  • Scope: Only DNS traffic is routed through the local VPN tunnel. Your general internet traffic (web browsing data, video streaming, personal messages, etc.) is NOT intercepted, monitored, or stored by the app.

3. Traffic Logs and Local Storage

The "Traffic" feature within the app provides visibility into the domain names being resolved:

  • Local Only: All traffic logs are stored strictly on your device's local memory (RAM/Cache).
  • No Remote Transmission: These logs are never sent to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • Auto-Deletion: Logs are permanently deleted when you clear them manually or when the application is closed.

4. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

To keep the application free for everyone, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements:

  • AdMob may use anonymous identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • You can review Google's data practices by visiting their Privacy & Terms page.

5. Third-Party DNS Providers

When you select a DNS provider (e.g., Google DNS, Cloudflare, OpenDNS), your DNS queries are subject to that provider's own privacy policy. We encourage you to review the policies of any DNS service you choose to use.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact the developer through the official app store channels.
